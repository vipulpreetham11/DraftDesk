# DraftDesk — Brand Guidelines

---

## 1. Brand overview

**Name:** DraftDesk
**Tagline:** Your content, scripted & tracked.
**One-liner:** AI-powered content planner and tracker for solo creators.

### Brand personality
Friendly · Clear · Encouraging

### Visual direction
**Warm Studio** — Warm coral tones with cream backgrounds. Inviting workspace for creators.

---

## 2. Color system

### Primary
- Primary: `#E8734A` (CTAs, links)
- Primary Light: `#F4A98B` (Hover, badges)
- Primary Dark: `#C45A32` (Pressed)

### Secondary
- Charcoal: `#2D2A26` (Headings)
- Charcoal Light: `#4A4540` (Subheadings)

### Accent
- Amber: `#F5C563` (Highlights, badges)
- Amber Light: `#FCE8B8` (Tag fills)

### Neutrals
- 50 (Page BG): `#FAF8F5`
- 100 (Card BG): `#F3F0EB`
- 200 (Borders): `#E8E4DE`
- 300 (Disabled): `#C9C4BC`
- 600 (Body): `#5C5750`
- 800 (Headings): `#2D2A26`
